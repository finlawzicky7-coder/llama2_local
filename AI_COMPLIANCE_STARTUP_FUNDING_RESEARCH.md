## 1. CLOUD CREDITS (Easiest to Get)

These are the **least competitive** opportunities — most accept nearly all qualifying startups. Apply to all of them and stack credits across providers.

### NVIDIA Inception Program (APPLY FIRST)
- **Cost:** Free, no equity
- **Credits:** Free GPU/cloud credits, preferred hardware pricing, DLI training credits
- **Bonus:** Acceptance unlocks additional credits from AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and Scaleway
- **Eligibility:** At least 1 developer, working website, incorporated, <10 years old
- **Why apply first:** Gateway to unlocking partner credits from all major cloud providers
- **Apply:** https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/startups/

### Microsoft for Startups (Founders Hub)
- **Credits:** $1,000 initially (90-day validity), then $4,000 more (180-day validity) for unfunded startups. Up to $100,000 with VC referral code.
- **Extras:** Free GitHub Enterprise (20 seats), Microsoft 365, Visual Studio, OpenAI credits, technical support
- **Barrier:** Lowest barrier of the big three cloud providers
- **Apply:** https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/startups

### AWS Activate
- **Founders Package:** $1,000 in credits + 1 year Business Support — no partner required
- **Portfolio Package:** Up to $100,000 in credits (2-year validity) through VCs, accelerators, or partners (Y Combinator, Techstars, Stripe Atlas, etc.)
- **Eligibility:** Under 10 years old, new to AWS Activate, pre-Series B
- **Apply:** https://aws.amazon.com/activate/

### Google Cloud for Startups
- **Bootstrap Tier:** Up to $2,000 in credits (unfunded/bootstrapped startups OK)
- **AI Startup Tier:** Up to $350,000 in credits for AI-first teams
- **Standard Tier:** Up to $200,000 in credits over 2 years
- **Free trial:** $300 for all new users
- **Apply:** https://cloud.google.com/startup

### Oracle Cloud (Always-Free GPU Tier)
- **Credits:** Always-free GPU tier available
- **Barrier:** Very low, sign up directly
- **Apply:** https://www.oracle.com/cloud/free/

**TOTAL POTENTIAL CLOUD CREDITS: $500,000+** across all providers combined.

## 2. FREE EQUIPMENT & HARDWARE PROGRAMS

### Lenovo Evolve Small Initiative
- **Grant:** $25,000 cash + $10,000 AI technology package (hardware/software)
- **Eligibility:** Small businesses under 75 employees, revenues under $7.5M, North America (excluding Quebec)
- **Competition level:** Moderate — targets small businesses broadly, not just tech startups
- **Apply:** https://www.lenovo.com/evolve

### NASE Growth Grants
- **Grant:** Up to $4,000 for hardware, software, training, marketing
- **Application window:** January–March each year
- **Competition level:** Low-moderate — $1M awarded since 2006
- **Apply:** https://www.nase.org/become-a-member/Membership-Benefits/business-development/growth-grants

### NVIDIA DGX Cloud Innovation Lab
- **What:** 60-day accelerator with hands-on GPU access (A100/H100)
- **Eligibility:** Must be NVIDIA Inception member first
- **Competition level:** Moderate — rolling applications, subject to availability
- **Apply:** Through NVIDIA Inception Portal after membership

## 4. FEDERAL GOVERNMENT GRANTS

### NSF SBIR/STTR — AI Topic Area
- **Phase I:** ~$250,000
- **Phase II:** ~$1,000,000+
- **Focus areas:** Deep learning-based AI systems, AI hardware, neuromorphic computing, smart edge devices
- **STATUS (Feb 2026):** Programs are in authorization lapse since Oct 1, 2025. Congress expected to reauthorize via must-pass spending/defense bill. **Monitor closely — new solicitations will reopen.**
- **Compliance angle:** New rules expected around cybersecurity diligence, foreign-entity screening, financial transparency
- **Website:** https://seedfund.nsf.gov/topics/artificial-intelligence/

### DoD SBIR/STTR
- **Same status as NSF** — in authorization lapse but expected to restart
- **Relevance:** AI compliance/governance directly relevant to defense procurement
- **Website:** https://www.dodsbirsttr.mil/

### DOE SBIR FY 2026
- **Active solicitations** for FY 2026
- **Website:** https://science.osti.gov/sbir/Funding-Opportunities/FY-2026

### Grants.gov (Central Federal Portal)
- **What:** Searchable database of all federal grant opportunities across 26 agencies
- **Cost:** Free to search and apply
- **Website:** https://www.grants.gov/

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

- **Start with cloud credits** — they're nearly guaranteed and worth $500K+ combined
- **AI Grant (aigrant.com)** is the single highest-value accessible grant ($850K total value, no credentials required)
- **NVIDIA Inception is the gateway** — join first to unlock partner benefits everywhere
- **SBIR/STTR is in limbo** but expected to restart — prepare applications now
- **AI compliance is a hot space** for investors and grant-makers due to EU AI Act and emerging US regulations
- **Stack everything** — there's no rule against holding credits/grants from multiple programs simultaneously
- **Use grant-finding tools** like Portkey, OpenGrants, and Granter.ai to discover niche opportunities specific to your state/city

**Estimated total accessible funding: $1M–$2M+ in non-dilutive grants, credits, and equipment**
